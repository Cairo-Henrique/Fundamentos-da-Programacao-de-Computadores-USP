# Fundamentals-of-Computer-Programming-USP

Repository containing the solutions of the exercise lists from the course Fundamentals of Computer Programming (SME0332), offered by the Institute of Physics of São Carlos (IFSC-USP).

Professor and author of the exercises: Roberto Federico Ausas

---

## Exercise Lists Overview

### **List 1 — Programming Basics**

Introduction to Python programming: variables, conditionals, loops, functions, vectors and matrices, random numbers, simple numerical calculations, and basic plotting.

### **List 2 — Monte Carlo & Random Processes**

Stochastic simulations including Monte Carlo integration, numerical estimation of π, random walks in 2D/3D, probability experiments, and statistical analysis with visualizations.

### **List 3 — Iterative Methods**

Iterative numerical algorithms such as recurrence relations, fixed-point iteration, Jacobi method for linear systems, logistic maps, and relaxation methods applied to physical systems.

### **List 4 — Varied Topics**

Image processing, advanced plotting and animation, sorting and searching algorithms, geometric and graph-based problems, and small computational physics projects.
